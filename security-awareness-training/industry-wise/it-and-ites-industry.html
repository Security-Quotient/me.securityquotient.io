Industry-wise

Security awareness for IT and ITeS industry

Fully customisable solutions

The high-tech IT and ITeS sector is the treasure trove that attracts cyber criminals.

By its very nature, IT and ITes employees use the latest in gadgets and software. Some of these devices and software may be at an early stage of development and hence vulnerable. Further, the work culture promotes innovation and creativity. This means, usage of personal devices and free access to Internet.

Hence, CISO and Cyber Security managers from the IT and ITeS industry have a tough challenge. They must balance cyber security with innovation. The game is between restriction and access.
