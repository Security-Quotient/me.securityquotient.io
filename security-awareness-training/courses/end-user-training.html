Description

Security Awareness alone is not enough. Employees must gain Cybersecurity Competence to defend the organisation from vicious Cybercrime.

Security Quotient has skillfully crafted this e-Learning course by using the time-tested method of Learning by Doing. This method is implemented using engaging, interactive 3D Cyber Risk simulations that enables the learner to encounter realistic Risk Scenarios and provides for immersive, experiential learning that builds memories. These memories get triggered when confronted with similar risks in the real world.


Course Overview

The recent proliferation of phishing and ransomware attacks are sufficient proof that cybercriminals are targeting employees more than systems. While most courses equip employees with awareness, this course takes a landmark step beyond by delivering skill training along with awareness to develop Cybersecurity competence.

The course starts with an overview of the latest Cyber-Attacks risks. The learner will proceed to encounter interactive 3D risk scenarios for an immersive risk solving experience. Following this experience, the learner will cover essential best practices.

The e-Learning course ends with a mandatory 10 question assessment that the learner must score a minimum of 80% to pass. Learners who fail will be guided to retake the assessment.
